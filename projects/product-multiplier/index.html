The Product Multiplier Playbook
From backlog babysitter to outcome amplifier
01
AI Fluency
  • Prompts → Workflows → Evaluations
  • Use AI as a collaborator, not an assistant
  • Automate research, specs, and reviews
  • Connect multiple tools into working systems
  • Evaluate quality, guardrails, cost, and latency
02
Data Intuition
  • Spot signals quickly and test faster
  • Form hypotheses instead of relying on hunches
  • Use actionable metrics over vanity stats
  • Close the loop with instrumented events
  • Base decisions on evidence, not opinion
AMPLIFY OUTCOMES
Don’t just manage requirements. Multiply results.
03
Systems Thinking
  • See product, tech, and business as one system
  • Map incentives and feedback loops
  • Set smart defaults that guide behavior
  • Design with trade-offs and safe exits in mind
  • Anticipate constraints and plan for resilience
04
Cross‑Domain Literacy
  • Import ideas from psychology, economics, and design
  • Innovate at the intersections of disciplines
  • Systematically borrow and remix patterns
  • Turn cross-field insights into experiments
  • Triangulate truth across multiple sources
05
Storytelling & Experimentation
  • Make the complex feel inevitable
  • Prototype boldly, cheaply, and often
  • Treat mistakes as multipliers
  • Communicate clearly and show working demos
  • Keep experimentation continuous and visible
